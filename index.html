Inter Community Interaction

We foster positive inter-community experiences and inter-ethnic reconciliation by engaging young women from North and South Mitrovica in long-term joint collaborative work.

Empower Young Women with high-value skills

When one woman helps another, amazing things can happen. The project equips 20 young women from Kosovo Albanian and Kosovo Serbian communities from South and North Mitrovica with high-value skills and hands on experience to use new technologies for social good.

Real World Digital Products

Digitalization is the 'integration of digital technology into everyday life'. We develop a digital tool that serves to contribute to better relations between communities which will serve the local populations for years to come.

About Connecting Hearts

The two dominant communities in Kosovo, Albanians and Serbians, continue to live side by side while inhabiting two separate worlds.
Interactions remain sporadic and happen mostly out of necessity rather than because of a genuine will to engage in constructive interaction.

Hence, the project addresses the identified challenges by adopting an innovative approach that leverages the use of new technologies to empower young women and foster positive inter-community interaction.

Activities

Foster inter-community, reconciliaton and tolerance through innovative uses of technology.

  • Digital tool development workshops

    A series of workshops that will span over three (3) months and will be delivered over two weekends each month. The young women will work in four (4) (ethnically) mixed teams each composed of five (5) participants.

  • "Connecting Hearts Through Technology" Hackathon

    A weekend-long hackathon on designing and developing prototypes of digital tools that facilitate inter-community interaction between inhabitants of North and South Mitrovica.

  • Development of digital tool facilitating inter-community interaction

    The winning digital tool voted by participants themselves will be fully developed into a real-world digital product.

  • Digital Tool Launch

    Launch to the public via two events held in North and South Mitrovica, during which citizens will be able to explore the platform’s features, meet with its creators, and engage in a discussion over its purpose.

  • Showcasing examples of inter-community reconciliation and participants work

    In addition to informing about the project itself, the project website will also serve to present stories, anectodotes or quotations of participants relating to their experience.

  • Digital Tool Promotion

    The use of the digital tool will be promoted via the social media presence of GCK, traditional media as well as by engaing the participants in disseminating information about the tool in their local communities.
